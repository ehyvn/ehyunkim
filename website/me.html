Me

Hi! I'm Ehyun Kim.

I grew up in Seoul, South Korea, and lived in many different places such as New Zealand, Jeju Island, and Louisiana. The multi-cultural experience developed the diverse perspective I have today. Art helped me understand myself better through the times where I was confused of my identity and where I belonged. That's the reason why the majority of my artworks consist of self portraits.

During my gap year, through an intensive design class, several design internships, and a mobile application coding project, I discovered my passion for human-centered-design. Moving forward, I strive to better the lives of people like us through the use of art and technology.
